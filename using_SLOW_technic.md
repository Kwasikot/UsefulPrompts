**Generate 5 pairs of real-life scenarios (10 total) contrasting reactive vs. mindful behavior.**

**Structure for each pair:**

1.  **Event:** Short trigger description.
    
2.  **Scenario A (Autopilot):** Describe the impulsive reaction and its negative short/long-term consequences.
    
3.  **Scenario B (SLOW):** Describe the transition through:
    
    - **S (Slow Down):** Physical signal detection + pause.
        
    - **L (Let Go):** Releasing tension/breath.
        
    - **O (Observe):** Cognitive distancing from thoughts/emotions.
        
    - **W (Wise Response):** Action based on **VaLoR** (Values, Long-term goals, Reason).
        

**Constraints:**

- Use everyday social/work/internal contexts.
    
- Tone: Neutral, rational, non-preachy.
    
- Format: Grouped in pairs (A then B).
    
- Language: Russian.
